THE GOLDEN ERA

Echoes of an Empire

From the 9th to 15th century, the Khmer Empire was the most powerful force in SOuthease Asia. Their engineering marvel, sophisticated irrigation systems, and divine architecture at Angkor continue to baffle and inspire the modern world.

Sacred Geometry

Temple layouts reflecting the Hindu Buddist cosmology, built with mathematical precision.

Hydraulic Master
